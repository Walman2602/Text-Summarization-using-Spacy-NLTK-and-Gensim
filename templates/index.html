Text Summarization

Text summarization or commonly called text summarization is one of the materials that aims to make it easier for readers to find information quickly. This is indicated by the increasing number of people browsing as a form of the need for faster and less time-consuming information, but it is not possible for every user to browse or read the entire contents of the article. It is this that must be considered, therefore a method that allows users to quickly find and browse information in articles. Single-article summaries have been the subject of focus in recent years where compression, speed redundancy, and section selection are critical to the formation of useful summaries. With this article that discusses Text Summarization, it is hoped that it can help readers understand the basic concepts of text summarization, types of summarization of text summarization and approaches to text summarization.
